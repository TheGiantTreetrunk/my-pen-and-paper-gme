How to Play

Welcome to Rage 'n' Riches! A game where you explore an ever changing dungeon filled with challenges, loot, and enemies.

You, the HERO!

@

Your objective is to survive as long as you can. The farther you get the better the loot and the more dangerous the challenges await you! There are nine classes total, each with different stats and play styles.

The Dungeon

This dungeon is large; infinitely large to be specific. No matter how many times you visit it will always be different. Always expect the unexpected...

Equipment

You will always start with your trusty long sword. Very durable and usable you will nevertheless find better weapons, shields and armor as you progress. BUT REMEMBER! Certain items require certain equipment to be used. Always choose wisely as it can make or even break your run...

Traps and Challenges

Throughout your run you will encounter many situations whether its traps that you've accidentially triggered or a challenge. This dungeon is ruthless and not for the weak...

Combat

No matter how you cut it with your kit you will be able to test your grit against all sorts of monsters throughout the dungeon some more powerful than others.

White = Common
Green = Uncommon
Red = Dangerous
Purple = Extremely Dangerous

A Royal Summons (of Questionable Safety)

Date: The Year of our King, 1402

To: The Brave, the Bold, and the Highly Expendable

The King’s Decree

By the Grace of the Crown and the pressing emptiness of the Royal Treasury, you are hereby "invited" to provide your services to the Realm. His Majesty has noted—with great interest and a small amount of impatience—that the Dungeon of the Mad Mage remains offensively full of gold while the palace wine cellar is looking dreadfully thin.

Your Royal Mandate

  • Extract the Shiny: Any gold, gems, or artifacts not bolted to the floor (and some that are) must be recovered for "safekeeping."
  • Survive the Decor: Please refrain from offending the portraits. The last hero we sent spent three hours listening to a Duke’s oil painting complain about the humidity. It was... unproductive.
  • Appease the Forge: Should you find the sentient forge, try to agree with its creative choices. If it wants to turn your sword into a very sharp ladle, show some gratitude.
  • Mind the Architecture: Try your best not to be digested by the masonry, wardrobes, or any chests that appear to have teeth.

Official Liability Waiver

"Brave the dungeon, retrieve the treasures, and for the love of the gods, try not to get eaten by the floorboards."

Be advised: the Crown shall not be held liable for any bruises, existential crises, or accidental kidnappings by vengeful floral families. Report to the dungeon entrance at dawn. Bring your own bandages; the King is currently using the royal supply to patch a drafty window in the throne room.

For the Glory of the Realm (and the King’s Pocketbook),

Lord Pendergast
High Scribe and Master of "Voluntary" Recruitment

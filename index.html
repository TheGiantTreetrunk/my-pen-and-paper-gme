Welcome to my project!

The year is 1402, and the King, in his infinite wisdom (and a desperate need for more gold), has issued a royal decree. All able-bodied adventurers are to delve into the depths of the Mad Mage's dungeon, a place as notorious for its riches as it is for its utter absurdity.

They say the dungeon itself is alive, a mischievous and malevolent entity that delights in subverting expectations. Heroes expecting to find simple booby traps will instead find themselves apologizing to insulted portraits that have stepped out of their frames. A simple supply closet might contain a self-conscious skeleton, and a healing herb might just summon its entire, very angry, floral family to defend it.

Within these ever-shifting walls, brave (or foolish) souls can find armories stocked with the finest plate and steel, and forges capable of creating legendary equipment. But beware the sentient forge that might have its own opinion on what it wants to create, or the treasure chest that's actually a very grumpy, and very toothy, mimic.

The King's command is simple: brave the dungeon, retrieve its treasures, and try not to get eaten by the architecture.

Game Features:

  • Character RPG Classes and Item Customization
  • Quests and Challenges
  • An ever shifting dungeon...
  • Countless enemies to best!
  • A rich and extensive item system.
  • In-depth crafting and professions.
  • Player housing and guilds.
  • A very hungry dungeon.
  • A sassy and omnipotent dungeon master.

# Scoring System Draft

## Consumables
*   Gold: +1 point per gold piece
*   Food: +5 points
*   Water: +5 points
*   Potion: +10 points
*   Arrows: +2 points per arrow
*   Bullets: +5 points per bullet
*   Scrolls: +15 points

## Equipment (Base Score, can be modified by rarity/tier)
*   Weapon: +25 points
*   Armor: +20 points
*   Shield: +15 points

## Combat
*   Defeating a regular enemy: +10 points
*   Defeating a boss: +100 points

## Progression
*   Clearing a room (all objects looted/enemies defeated): +50 points
*   Clearing a section (all rooms in a section cleared): +200 points

## Penalties (Optional)
*   Smashing an object instead of opening it: -5 points
